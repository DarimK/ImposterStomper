Battle against the imposters and beat the imposter boss at stage 100! To defeat an imposter, jump up and stomp on it for as long as possible.

Single player: allows you to fight the imposters in story mode. Use the platforms to jump up to the next stage!

PVP: allows you to battle a friend to see who is the strongest Imposter Stomper!

How to move: WASD, arrow keys, or click and drag joystick.

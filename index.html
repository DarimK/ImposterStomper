Imposter Stomper



Battle against the imposters and survive for as long as you can! To defeat an imposter, jump up and stomp on it for as long as possible.

Single player: allows you to fight the imposters in stages
PVP: allows you to battle a friend to see who is the strongest Imposter Stomper!

Player 1 movement: WASD
Player 2 movement: arrow keys
